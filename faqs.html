How do I book a trip?
You can book a trip by visiting our packages page, choosing your preferred package, and following the easy steps to book.
Are there any special offers or discounts available?
Yes! We have exclusive discounts and offers throughout the year. Please visit our Special Offers page to learn more.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes, we offer special group rates for larger parties. Please contact our customer support for more details.
What is the cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package. Please refer to the specific terms and conditions on the package page or contact us for more information.
Can I customize my trip?
Yes, we offer customized trips to suit your preferences. You can contact our support team for assistance in planning your perfect trip.

The price of each package includes accommodation, transportation, meals, and guided tours. However, optional activities and personal expenses are not included.
How do I contact customer support?
You can contact us through our Contact Us page or directly email us at info@travelx.com. Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
